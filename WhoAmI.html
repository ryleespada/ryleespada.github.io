Who Am I?

I'm a librarian and archivist with experience across academic special collections, research libraries, community archives, and nonprofit art collections. My areas of interest include:

  1. critical and specialized cataloging
  2. preservation of rare print and art materials
  3. metadata design
  4. digital archiving
  5. research and reference support

A Bit About My Background

When I was younger, I spent much of my time bouncing back and forth between studies in statistical research and mathematics and my training as an actor, singer, and director. Although I haven't performed (or done multi-variable calculus) in years, those early experiences informed my organizational skills, interdisciplinary approach to cultural histories, and love of the arts—from gallery works to ephemeral performances.
